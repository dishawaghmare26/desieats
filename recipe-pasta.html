Creamy Garlic Pasta

A rich and decadent pasta dish that comes together in under 30 minutes. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

Easy
25 mins
4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) Fettuccine or your favorite pasta
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 5 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper, freshly ground
  • 1/4 cup fresh Parsley, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Pasta water, as needed

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Just before draining, reserve 1 cup of the starchy pasta water.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, melt butter in a large skillet or pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes. Be careful not to burn it.
  3. Slowly pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing it to thicken slightly.
  4. Reduce heat to low and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the sauce. Toss to combine, ensuring every strand is coated. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water until it reaches your desired consistency.
  6. Stir in the fresh parsley. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese if desired.
